About Us

Welcome to Al's On The Go, where community and delicious food collide! Owned by the dedicated Helene Smiley, our food truck is not just a place to grab a quick bite but a hub of warmth and connection inspired by the legacy of Al, Helene's father.

Nestled within the heart of East Stroudsburg, specifically within Dansbury Park, we take pride in serving up mouthwatering meals alongside a generous side of camaraderie. Following in the footstep of Al, Helene embodies the spirit of giving back, actively participating in local initiatives, supporting youth organizations, and fostering partnerships with neighboring businesses.

For over 7 years, Al's On The Go has been a beacon of culinary consistency and community engagement. Whether you're stopping by for a savory snack or a hearty meal, our mission remains unchanged: to deliver fast, fun, and friendly food experiences that you can always rely on!

Our Menu

Discover the mouthwatering delights awaiting you on our menu at Al's On The Go! Crafted with love and care by our owner, Helene Smiley, our selection of appetizers and meals is sure to tantalize your taste buds.

From homemade classics to innovative creations, each dish is prepared with fresh, quality ingredients and a dash of culinary expertise. Our menu evolves with the seasons, ensuring that every visit is a delightful adventure filled with new flavors and experiences.

At Al's On The Go, we understand that dietary needs vary, which is why we happily accommodate any alterations necessary to meet your preferences and restrictions. Whether you're craving a quick bite or planning to share with friends, our menu offers something for everyone.

Indulge in the flavors of community and camaraderie with every bite at Al's On The Go. Join us and experience the joy of good food, good company, and good times!

Al's Water Ice

Quench your thirst and beat the heat with the refreshing taste of Al's Water Ice! As spring blooms into summer and the temperatures rise, there's no better way to cool down than with our tantalizing array of flavors.

From classic favorites to exotic surprises, our water ice lineup offers something for every palate. Indulge in the timeless sweetness of lemon, savor the tropical tang of mango, or dive into the lusciousness of strawberry. Craving something adventurous?
